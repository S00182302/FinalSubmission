SYG’s focus is to pair up volunteer’s skills and young people’s interests. Volunteers can apply to volunteer online (pending Garda clearance) and select which day/days they would like to commit to attending. They can also add a list of skills they would be willing to share with young people.

The club house is open on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays between 3pm and 5pm. Two volunteers are required for the premises to open. The premise facilities include: pool tables, games consoles, darts, film and more. The skills of the volunteer who has committed to attend on that day, is listed on the website in the Activities Calendar.

This allows members (young people) to stay informed about when relevant activities are available. Typical activities include music, art, dance, film, console games, etc.

SYG are also engaged with the community in local community projects. These projects tend to take place outside of club house hours. Projects are suggested by members and the management committee who are in contact with local council services.
